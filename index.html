Our Products

Zeus

Experience the traditional taste of mate with our Wooden Mate. Handcrafted from high-quality wood, this mate gourd is perfect for enjoying your favorite mate blends. Its natural texture and unique grains add a touch of elegance to your mate-drinking experience.

Atenas

Experience the harmonious blend of flavors with our Pumpkin Mate cured with mandarin and orange peel. This mate gourd is expertly crafted from cured pumpkin, resulting in a unique taste profile. The infusion of mandarin and orange peel adds a refreshing citrusy twist to the traditional mate, creating a delightful and invigorating drinking experience.

Arquimedes

Indulge in the rich and smoky flavors of our Whisky-Infused Mate. This unique mate gourd is crafted from cured wood that has been infused with the essence of whisky. The result is a distinctive blend of traditional mate and the enticing aroma of whisky, creating a truly exceptional drinking experience.
